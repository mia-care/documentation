SaaS Deployment

P4SaMD offers two cloud-hosted deployment options: Multi-tenant SaaS and Dedicated SaaS. Both are fully managed by Mia-Care — you never need to provision servers, manage databases, or handle platform upgrades.

Multi-tenant SaaS​

In the multi-tenant model, your organization runs alongside other P4SaMD customers on a shared cloud infrastructure. Your data is kept strictly isolated through row-level security at the database layer and organization-scoped access control at the application layer — no user from another organization can ever see or access your data.

This mode is the fastest way to get started: provisioning a new organization takes minutes. Platform updates are applied automatically during scheduled maintenance windows, so you always run the latest version without any action on your part.

The features available to your organization depend on your subscription plan.

Dedicated SaaS​

In the dedicated model, Mia-Care provisions a private cloud environment — a dedicated Kubernetes cluster, dedicated database, and dedicated object storage — exclusively for your organization. No other customer shares any part of your infrastructure.

Dedicated SaaS is available starting from the Professional plan. It is the recommended choice when:

  • Your organization requires contractual data-isolation guarantees beyond logical row-level separation.
  • You need a custom domain (e.g., samd.yourcompany.com) with a TLS certificate issued to your domain.
  • You have specific data-residency requirements (e.g., data must remain within a specific EU region).
  • Your procurement or compliance process requires a dedicated-infrastructure vendor commitment.

Platform updates are applied during a scheduled maintenance window agreed with you — giving your team advance notice before any change is deployed.

System Requirements​

P4SaMD SaaS is a fully managed, browser-based web application. There is nothing to install and no servers, databases, or Kubernetes for you to provision — that is all handled by Mia-Care. (Infrastructure requirements such as Kubernetes, PostgreSQL, object storage, and an OIDC provider apply only to On-premise deployments.) The only requirements are on the end-user device.

Applies to: P4SaMD v3.x  ·  Last updated: 30 June 2026

Onboarding Prerequisites​

Beyond the system requirements above, onboarding a new SaaS organization requires only a few things on your side.

Mandatory​

RequirementDetails
Business emailA reachable email address per user, for invitations, notifications, and password resets. Ensure mail from Mia-Care's sending domain is not blocked by your spam filters.
Organization administratorA named person to complete the initial organization setup wizard and manage members, roles, and settings.
Subscription planAn active subscription agreement with the plan tier that covers the features you need (see the plan comparison above).

Optional (plan-dependent)​

These unlock specific capabilities and are only needed if you intend to use the corresponding feature.

Getting Access​

To create a new SaaS organization, contact Mia-Care or speak to your account representative. Provisioning typically completes within one business day for multi-tenant and three to five business days for dedicated environments.

Once your organization is provisioned, you will receive a welcome email with your instance URL, admin credentials, and a link to this documentation.
